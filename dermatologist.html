Dermatology

Worried about dark spots? Acne? Some odd bumps on your arms that don't seem to go away? Contact a dermatologist!

Skin of all shapes and colors deserves necessary attention. Whether it something serious like skin cancer - yes, Black people get skin cancer too! - or something smaller like a bothersome scar on your cheek. A dermatologist is there to help you feel safe and comfortable in your skin.

In addition to dermatology services, there are things you can do at home to take care of your skin. Below are some resources that can supplement some of the information you can get from a dermatologist:

  • My Black is Beautiful: Black Girl's Guide to Great Skin
  • Elle: 22 Black Owned Skincare Brands to Support
  • Treasure Locks: How to Care for African American Skin
