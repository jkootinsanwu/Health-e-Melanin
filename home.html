Steps to Stop the Spread:

  • Stay 6ft from other people and wear a mask in public, covering mouth AND nose.
  • Wash your hands often.
  • Cover your cough and sneeze.
  • Avoid touching eyes, nose, mouth.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects.
  • Stay home when you are sick.

Covid-19 Testing

Before getting tested for COVID-19, consider these criteria:

  • Do you have symptoms of COVID-19?
  • Have you had close contact (within 6 feet of an infected person for at least 15 minutes) with someone with confirmed COVID-19?
  • Have you been asked or referred to get testing by a healthcare provider or health department?

Everyone does not NEED to be tested. If you do get tested, you should self-quarantine/isolate at home pending test results and follow the advice of your health care provider or a public health professional.

At Home Mask Creation

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) recommends that people "wear masks in public settings when around people who don’t live in your household, especially when other social distancing measures are difficult to maintain.”

Masks with at least 2 layers of fabric are most likely to reduce the spread of COVID-19 when they are widely used by people in public settings. Simple masks can be made at home using washable, breathable fabric and may help prevent the spread of COVID-19.
