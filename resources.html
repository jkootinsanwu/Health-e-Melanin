We still think you deserve access to affordable healthcare. Below are some resources that may be beneficial to you!

Community Health Centers

A low-cost health care option would be a community health center, where how much you pay depends on your income. They provide:

  • Prenatal Care
  • Baby Shots
  • General Primary Care
  • Referrals to specialized care, including mental health, substance abuse, and HIV/AIDS

If this sounds like a good option, you can locate a community health center through HealthCare.Gov here
